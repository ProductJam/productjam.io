ProductJam! #8

Monday 21st October 2019

After a slightly longer than expected summer break we're back and in this session you can set the agenda with the things you want to talk about.

We'll be running a 'lean coffee' session where you can propose topics for discussion at the start, then we vote on the order we want to talk about them.

Whether you're new to this thing called "product" and want to get your head around it, or you've been doing it for years and have a few questions for other people, nothing is off limits - so get your thinking cap on and come along! The sort of things we might talk about are:

  • How should I price my product?
  • What are the right sort of metrics I should be thinking about?
  • How can I attract and retain a great team?
  • Should I make my roadmap public?
  • How do I get started?

Please join us from 18:00 with a bit of networking, then we'll get into the session from about 18:30. Refreshments will be provided!

We look forward to seeing you there!
